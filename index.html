Player access — PLL pros attract elite youth as spectators. A camp the same weekend puts Core in the same city as 10,000+ lacrosse fans.

Coach access — D1 coaches attend PLL games. A clinic while coaches are in town creates natural overlap.

Media synergy — PLL draws IL and TLN. Core events in the same city create co-coverage opportunities.

Travel logistics — Families booking hotels for PLL weekends — a co-located camp means one trip, two events.
