// THREE CRITICAL INSIGHTS //
1. Sep–Oct "No Lax Evals" — Coaches CANNOT attend lacrosse events for evaluation. Every fall tournament has zero D1 recruiting value. Youth events are the correct product.

2. November 1 First-Mover — The NCAA unlocks lacrosse evaluations on November 1. Coaches blacked out for 2 months. First event captures pent-up demand. Core's single biggest fall opportunity.

3. IMLCA Windows — Two exceptions: Dec 10–14 (Convention) and Jul 1–10 (July Meeting). Pacific Elite capitalizes in CA — East Coast equivalent UNCLAIMED.
